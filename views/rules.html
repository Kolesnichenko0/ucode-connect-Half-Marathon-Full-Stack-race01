In this game, two players fight each other using cards representing various heroes from the minecraft world. The main goal of the game is to reduce the opponent's health to zero.

Basic Rules:

    Health: Each player starts with 40 health points.
    Cards: Each card has unique characteristics.
    Turn Order: Players take turns playing cards to attack or defend.
    Coins: For each turn, players earn 2 gold bars, which can be used later to buy cards.
    Heroes: Heroes can attack the enemy while protecting the player's health.

How to Play:

    Each player shuffles his deck and takes the initial 3-card combination.
    Players take turns playing cards using their gold bars.
    Attacks can be directed at the opponent or at his cards.
    The game continues until the health of one of the players reaches zero.
    The player whose opponent loses all health wins the game.
